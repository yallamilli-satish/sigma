OUR COMPANY

Sigma Seafoods, established in 2020, is a dedicated processing unit specializing in the processing and export of high-quality shrimp.

Excellence & Quality

With a focus on excellence, we ensure that our products meet stringent quality standards, providing our clients with the finest shrimp products available. Our commitment to sustainable practices underscores our mission to deliver both exceptional taste and environmental responsibility.

Global Reach

Our products are exported to a diverse range of global markets, including the USA, EU, China, Middle East, and numerous other countries. This broad reach reflects our ability to meet the varied demands of international clientele while maintaining the highest standards of product excellence and customer satisfaction.

Premium Waters

We farm and process premium seafood products from pristine waters of eastern coast of India. Our farming operations started in 2010 and now it is extended to an area of almost 1500 acres across beautiful state of Andhra Pradesh.

State-of-the-Art Processing

From farming we ventured in to selling frozen shrimp in Indian market from 2015 with eye on global markets for our premium quality shrimp. The state-of-the-art processing plant is located in Peddapuram. Proximity of raw material sources to our processing plant guarantees maximum freshness. Our team is dedicated to provide our customers with top-quality products.
